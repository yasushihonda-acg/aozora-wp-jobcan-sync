OUTFIT (default — BLACK POLO SHIRT with BLUE lanyard):
- Solid BLACK short-sleeve polo shirt with a soft collar and a 2-3 button placket (buttons visible or partially undone). Plain single-color (pure black or very dark charcoal).
- NO piping, NO trim, NO accent color on the collar, placket, or sleeves. The polo is completely black.
- Optionally one subtle chest pocket on the left side (small, matching the baseline).
- BRIGHT COBALT-BLUE lanyard around the neck (bright cobalt / royal blue, clearly visible against the black polo) with a transparent-case rectangular ID badge hanging at chest level.
- IMPORTANT: The character's identity (face, hair, glasses, skin tone) must match the reference exactly. Only the scene, action, secondary props, and accessories change according to the job type below.

OUTFIT VARIATIONS (allowed when the scene calls for it — face / hair / glasses must still match):
- Office: black polo with a neutral charcoal or beige cardigan open over it (cardigan itself is a subdued neutral tone, no bright accent).
- Management / consultant: tailored jacket in a neutral muted tone (charcoal / dark navy / beige) worn open over the black polo.
- Casual visit / blog: black polo only, lanyard optional.
- Home-visit nurse: black polo with a small crossbody bag or medical kit, blue lanyard maintained.
- IT: tailored dark-navy or charcoal blazer open over the black polo, blue lanyard maintained.

ACCESSORIES RULES BY JOB TYPE (介護業界の一般的な服装規定に準拠):
- **HOOP / DANGLE 系ピアスは全職種で FORBIDDEN** (認知症の利用者に引っ張られる risk、医療安全、印象上の unprofessionalism、いずれも共通)。生成物にこれが描画された場合は再生成。
- **care / care-2 / care-3 (介護スタッフ)**: earring は描画されない方が理想。もし描画されても極小 stud (close to earlobe, dot-sized, no dangle) までは industry で許容範囲。**hoop / drop / dangle は絶対 NG**。NO rings, NO bracelets, NO wristwatch。爪短く、髪は束ねる。
- **nurse (看護師 / 訪問看護)**: 同上。earring は描画されない方が理想、極小 stud まで許容、hoop/dangle 絶対 NG。simple medical-appropriate wristwatch OK if the scene calls for it。
- **consultant / consultant-2 (相談員 / ケアマネジャー)**: Small subtle stud earring OK (close to earlobe, no dangle). Simple watch OK. No rings on active-work hand.
- **office / office-2 (事務)**: Small hoop or drop earring OK (subtle). Watch OK. Small subtle rings OK.
- **it (IT / システム)**: Small hoop or drop earring OK. Watch OK.
- **default (fallback)**: Follow care rules (safest, most professional appearance for eldercare industry).
- **philosophy / flow illustration** (multi-person scenes): Character defaults to no earring or 極小 stud in care-worker context; if she is in a consultative role in the scene, small stud is OK.

CORPORATE COLOR PALETTE (subtle integration, not mechanical flat blocks):
- Primary blue accent: `#00C4CC` — use on the lanyard, secondary interior accents (a door panel, a wall stripe, a chair back), or small props. Should feel natural, not forced.
- Grey: `#575656` — for text-like small props, subtle shadow tones.
- Beige: `#f8f5ee` — for interior wall bases, warm ivory backgrounds.

FORBIDDEN:
- V-neck scrubs (this is the 2026-07-01 OLD Phase 1.5 style, superseded by polo shirt 2026-07-02).
- Turquoise / teal / cyan colored polos or scrubs (this is the 2026-06-29 OLDEST baseline).
- White piping or trim on the polo.
- White or grey lanyard (must be blue).
- Bright accent colors on the polo itself (only the lanyard and subtle interior accents are bright).
- Dangling earrings, large hoops, or facial piercings in ANY job type (unprofessional for eldercare industry).
- Rings on care/nurse's active-work hand.
- Long or brightly-colored nails.
