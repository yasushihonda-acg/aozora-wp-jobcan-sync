OUTFIT (default — BLACK V-neck scrub with BLUE lanyard):
- Solid BLACK V-neck scrub top, short sleeves, plain single-color (pure black or very dark charcoal).
- NO piping, NO trim, NO accent color on the collar or sleeves. The scrub is completely black.
- One chest pocket on the left side (subtle, matching the baseline).
- BLUE LANYARD around the neck (bright cobalt / royal blue, clearly visible against the black scrub) with a transparent-case ID badge hanging at chest level.
- IMPORTANT: The character's identity (face, hair, glasses, earring, skin tone) must match the reference exactly. Only the scene, action, and secondary props change.

OUTFIT VARIATIONS (allowed when the scene calls for it — face / hair / glasses / earring must still match):
- Office: black scrub with a neutral charcoal or beige cardigan over it (cardigan itself is a subdued neutral tone, no bright accent).
- Management / consultant: tailored jacket in a neutral muted tone (charcoal / dark navy / beige) worn open over the black scrub.
- Casual visit / blog: black scrub only, lanyard optional.
- Home-visit nurse: black scrub with a small crossbody bag or medical kit, blue lanyard maintained.

FORBIDDEN:
- Turquoise / teal / cyan colored scrubs (this is the OLD baseline, do not use).
- White piping or trim on the scrub.
- White or grey lanyard (must be blue).
- Bright accent colors on the shirt itself (only the lanyard is bright).
- Polo shirts (this is a scrub with V-neck, not a polo with button placket).
