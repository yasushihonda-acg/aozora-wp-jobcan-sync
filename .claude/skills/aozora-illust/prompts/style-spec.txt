ILLUSTRATION STYLE (must match the reference exactly):
- Polished anime/manga illustrated style — completed, magazine-quality look.
- Fine detailed linework — thin, deliberate, sharp lines. Not thick manga-outline, not sketchy.
- Subtle thoughtful shading — soft gradients on skin, glossy highlights on hair, gentle folds on clothing. NOT flat colors, NOT heavy 3D shading.
- Crisp clean edges — no watercolor bleed, no ink washing, no paper texture.
- Multiple small glossy highlights scattered across the black hair (indicating light reflection).
- Warm color temperature: warm peach skin, warm coral lips, dark black hair with brown warm highlights.
- Background: white or very pale minimal background (light beige, soft pale blue). Complex or busy backgrounds are FORBIDDEN. The character is the focus.
- Character composition: centered or slightly off-center portrait / half-body / 3/4 view. Occasional secondary character (elderly patient, colleague, applicant) is OK.
- Small details rendered with care: earring, lanyard clip, scrub pocket seam, tortoiseshell frame pattern.

FORBIDDEN:
- Transparent watercolor flat-wash (this is the OLD baseline style, do not use).
- Heavy washi / paper texture background.
- Over-saturated colors, oil-paint impasto.
- Anime-glossy oversized eyes with sparkles.
- Flat vector / infographic simplification.
- Specific artist name mimicry (e.g., Eguchi Hisashi, particular manga artists).
- Text, logos, or brand marks in the image.

NG examples to avoid:
- 透明水彩フラット塗り (旧 baseline)
- フラットベクター (illust-philosophy 旧版のような 3D-shape 単純化)
- 過度な線のにじみ・ぼかし
- キラキラ・ソフトフォーカス過剰
